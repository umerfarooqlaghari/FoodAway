Rescue good food. Save the planet.

Grabengo connects you with local restaurants, bakeries, cafés, and supermarkets offering surplus food at unbeatable prices. Every surprise bag you rescue prevents ~2.5 kg of CO₂ emissions — equivalent to charging a smartphone 300+ times.

HOW IT WORKS
• Browse nearby stores and discover surprise bags from local favorites
• Reserve fresh food, bakery items, meals, and more at ½ price or less
• Pick up at your chosen time slot
• Enjoy premium quality food while fighting food waste

✓ Save money — Get fresh, high-quality food at a fraction of retail price
✓ Save the planet — 1 in 3 meals globally are wasted. You can change that.
✓ Support local — Discover independent bakeries, cafés, restaurants, and businesses near you
✓ Surprise included — Unpack mystery bags from places you've never tried
✓ 4.9★ rating — Trusted by 2 million+ users worldwide

Fresh prepared meals, bakery items, sushi, salads, sandwiches, pizza, desserts, groceries, beverages, and much more — directly from your favorite local businesses.

THE IMPACT

Every rescue bag is a small act that creates global impact. Join the movement today.
